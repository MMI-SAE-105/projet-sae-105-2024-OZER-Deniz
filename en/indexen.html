Sonic Relics

Explore the sonic remnants of the present, rediscovered in a distant future

Sonic Relics is an immersive exploration of the evolution of music playback technologies, from the phonograph to modern streaming services like Spotify and Deezer. Through this platform, dive into the eras that shaped how we listen to music and discover how yesterday's innovations foreshadow tomorrow's sound technologies.

Key Features of Sonic Relics

  • Interactive Timeline: Explore the key stages in the evolution of music players
  • Exploration of Works: Discover works and projects tied to this incredible evolution
  • Future Technologies: Learn about upcoming sound innovations and speculations
